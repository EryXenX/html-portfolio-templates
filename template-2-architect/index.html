The Studio
02

Architecture as a discipline of listening.

Elara Voss Studio was founded on the belief that great buildings emerge from deep understanding — of site, of culture, and of the people who will inhabit the space. We resist trends in favour of timeless clarity.

Based in Amsterdam with projects across Europe and Southeast Asia, our practice operates at the intersection of material honesty and spatial innovation. We work with private clients, municipalities, and cultural institutions.
